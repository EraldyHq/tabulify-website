====== Data Generator ======

{{ :docs:generator:undraw_file_manager.svg?600&ratio=21:9 |}}

===== About =====
<icon name="mdi:cog"/> ''Data Generator'' is a  module that permits to generate data.

It is based on two elements:
  * the [[docs:resource:generator|generator resource]] that defines and generate data.
  * the [[docs:op:fill|data fill]] operation that ''fill'' [[docs:resource:content|content data resource]] with data.
