====== Data Operation - Print ======


===== About =====
''printing'' is the process to show the content of [[docs:resource:resource|data resources]] on a media.

The ''print'' operation is supported by:
  * the [[docs:tabul:data:print|data print command]]
  * the [[docs:tabul:data:head|data head command]]
  * the [[docs:tabul:data:tail|data tail command]]
