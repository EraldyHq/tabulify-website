====== Data Operation - Update ======


===== About =====
''update'' is a [[transfer|transfer]] [[op|data operation]] that will update one or more record of a [[docs:resource:content|content data resource]].


The [[docs:tabul:data:update|data update operation]] supports the ''update'' operation.
