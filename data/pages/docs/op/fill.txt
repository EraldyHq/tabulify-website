====== Data Operation - Fill ======


===== About =====
''fill'' is an [[op|data operation]] that creates or loads [[docs:generator:generator|generated data]] into a [[docs:resource:resource|data resource]].

<note>
This ''data operation'' can be performed with the [[docs:tabul:data:fill|tabul data fill command]] that pilots the [[docs:generator:generator|data generator]].
</note>
