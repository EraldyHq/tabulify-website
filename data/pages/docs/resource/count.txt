====== Data Resource - Count Attribute ======


===== About =====
''count'' is a [[attribute#common|common data resource attribute]] that gives the number of children.
  * In the case of a [[docs:resource:table|table]], the children are [[record|records]]
  * In the case of a [[docs:resource:directory|directory]], the children are contained [[file|files]] and/or [[directory|directories]].
