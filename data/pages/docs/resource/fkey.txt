====== Foreign Key (fkey)  ======


===== About =====
A ''foreign key'' represent a [[docs:resource:dependency|dependency relationship]] between [[docs:resource:table|tables]]. It's a [[docs:system:relational|relational concept]].

The [[docs:tabli:data:dep|data dep command]] show you the ''foreign key'' dependency.
