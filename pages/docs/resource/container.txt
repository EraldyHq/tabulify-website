====== Data Resource - Container Resource ======


===== About =====
A ''container resource'' is a [[resource|resource]] that contains other resources:
  * [[content|data resource]] ([[script|script resource]] included)
  * or another ''container resource''

[[schema|sql schema]] or a [[directory|directory]] are ''container resources''



===== Selection =====
A ''container resource'' is selected with a [[data_selector|data selector]].
